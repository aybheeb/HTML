Our Story

Founded in 2020, Two Spoons brings authentic halal cuisine to Toledo. Our journey began with a simple mission: to serve delicious, certified halal food that brings people together. Every dish we prepare combines traditional recipes with modern cooking techniques, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience.
