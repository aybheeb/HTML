Our Story

Founded in 2020, Two Spoons brings authentic halal cuisine to Toledo. Our journey began with a simple mission: to serve delicious, certified halal food that brings people together.

Our Halal Commitment

At Two Spoons, we take our halal commitment seriously. All our ingredients are sourced from certified halal suppliers, and our kitchen maintains strict halal standards. We proudly serve only zabiha halal meat, ensuring every dish meets the highest halal requirements. Our certification is renewed annually, and our staff receives regular training on halal food handling practices.
