Reservation Policies

General Policy

  • Reservations are held for 15 minutes past the booking time
  • Tables for 2-4 people are reserved for up to 2 hours
  • Tables for 5 or more are reserved for up to 2.5 hours

Large Group Bookings

  • Groups of 8-15 require a 20% deposit
  • For parties larger than 15, please contact us directly
  • Set menu options available for large groups

Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your reservation
  • Late cancellations may incur a fee
  • No-shows will forfeit their deposit
